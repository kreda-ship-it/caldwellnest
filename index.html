The problem we lived

Looking for housing or campus resources meant scrolling Facebook Marketplace, sending cold DMs to strangers, or hoping someone posted in an overflowing group chat. No way to verify anyone was a student. No moderation. No safety. Just chaos.

📴
Scattered across apps
GroupMe, Facebook, Snapchat — no one place to find housing, deals, or campus resources.
❌
No verification

Built for everyone else
